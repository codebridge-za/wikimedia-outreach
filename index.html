Wikimedia ZA

Design Indaba Outreach

Overview

As the local South African Wikimedia chapter, we try to encourage people to contribute African content to Wikipedia. We also understand that at times it might seem a daunting and time consuming task.

Knowing that many alumnus are notable and belong in Wikipedia, we’d like to offer our support to the Design Indaba to help create articles. Also to offer email support once questions arise.

Steps

      Create an account in Wikipedia.

      Upload relevant media such as photos including good descriptions.

      You have to have permission of the author or have to be your own. Also, you’ll have to use a permissive license and make sure to have understood the implication.

      Create an article draft as email body in your response or format most convenient to you (PDF, ODT, DOCX). Make sure it is detailed and includes plenty of reliable and verifiable references.

      Reply to designindaba@outreach.org.za which should include the article draft and the username

Feedback

Dependent on the feedback volume, we’ll review your feedback and be in touch via email. We guess within two weeks for article and within 4 days for questions.

The result of our combined efforts is a shiny new article and more African content. Yay!
